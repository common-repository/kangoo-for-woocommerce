== Installation ==
1. Kangoo for Woocommerce can be installed directly through your WordPress Plugins dashboard.
2. Click "Add New" and Search for "Kangoo for Woocommerce"
3. Install and Activate
4. In your admin panel or dashboard, go to `Woocommerce` -> `Settings` -> `Shipping` tab ->  `Kangoo Shipping` Section
5. Introduce your Kangoo Credentials and your Store location
6. Click Save, and your clients are ready to choose to ship your products using Kangoo

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How does this plugin work? =
Customers in your local area, right now its limited to Mami Dade and Broward County can order any item on your site and have it delivered to them same day thanks to Kangoo.

= Are there weight restrictions? =
Yes, 50lbs for car delivery.

= Are there any guarantees if a Kangoo Driver breaks the merchandise? =
Yes, Kangoo guarantees all deliveries.
